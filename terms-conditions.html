Cancellation & Return Policy
How can I cancel my Order?

You can cancel the order by calling our customer support 8639740102 or ping us on chat. We will not be able to cancel the order once it is out for delivery.

Can I Edit my Order?

Yes, you can edit the order by calling our customer support 8639740102 or ping us on chat within 15 mins of placing the order. The customer support agent can help you to add/remove individual items within the order.

Note
  • The partial refund cannot be accepted once your order is sent for processing.
  • Any modification on the order, the existing order will be cancelled and replaced with the new order number
Delivery
Is there a minium order?

Yes, there is a minimum bill value of Rs.149 excluding delivery charges.

What are the delivery charges?

We charge a nominal fee of Rs.19 for all scheduled orders and Rs. 39 for express deliveries.

Note

To avail schedule delivery, the customer has to place the order 30 mins prior to the delivery time. Express Delivery carries Rs. 39 charges and the order will be delivered within 30 mins from the ordering time. You can place the order under express delivery only between 7:00AM - 7:30PM.

What are your delivery timings?

We deliver every day of the week from 7:00am – 7:00pm.

Do I need to register or create an account before making a purchase?

You are free to browse our site, but when we deliver, we need to know who you are and where you stay. Therefore, we need you to register before making your purchase.

Hassle free COD orders
How can I use the UPI option?

Once the delivery executive reaches your location, you can ask him to show the QR code from his chickenbazar rider app, you can just scan the QR code using Google pay/Phone pay/ PayTM for paying the amount and enjoy the Hassle free COD.

Online Payment
Is my credit card information safe on your site?

Absolutely. All online payment related transactions are carried out through the secure payment gateway and are Payment Card Industry Certified (PCI DSS Certified).

Will I be charged any additional fee for using online payment option?

No, we don’t charge any additional fee.

My bank is not listed in the online payment option, how do I pay?

You can try paying through your Credit/Debit card, Online Wallets, PayU or through Cash on Delivery.
